About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival game where you must battle against waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Test your skills, upgrade your character, and see how long you can survive in this challenging environment.

With intuitive controls and engaging gameplay, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment for gamers of all skill levels. The game features stunning graphics, immersive sound effects, and addictive progression systems.

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character around the map. Automatically attack nearby monsters and collect experience points to level up. Choose powerful upgrades to enhance your abilities and survive longer.

The longer you survive, the more challenging the game becomes. Develop strategies to maximize your survival time and achieve high scores!
