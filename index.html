Monster Survivors

Survive against endless waves of monsters in this thrilling roguelike game!

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character around the map. Automatically attack nearby monsters and collect experience points to level up. Choose powerful upgrades to enhance your abilities and survive longer.

The longer you survive, the more challenging the game becomes. Develop strategies to maximize your survival time and achieve high scores!

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from various characters with unique abilities and playstyles.

Upgrade System

Collect experience and level up to unlock powerful abilities and enhancements.

Diverse Enemies

Face a wide variety of monsters with different attack patterns and abilities.

Challenging Bosses

Test your skills against powerful boss monsters that appear at regular intervals.
